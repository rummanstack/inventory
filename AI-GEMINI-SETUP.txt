AI GEMINI SETUP

Add these keys to backend/.env:

AI_PROVIDER=gemini
GEMINI_API_KEY=your_google_ai_studio_key_here
GEMINI_MODEL=gemini-3.5-flash
AI_MIN_SECONDS_BETWEEN_REQUESTS=5
AI_DAILY_LIMIT=100

Backend endpoints added:

GET /api/ai-insights/status
- Checks whether Gemini is configured.

POST /api/ai-insights/customers/:customerId/insight
- Requires retail customer view permission.
- Sends summarized customer purchase, due, return, loyalty, installment, and top-product data to Gemini.

POST /api/ai-insights/low-stock/advice
- Requires product view permission and low-stock-alerts feature.
- Sends summarized low-stock product data to Gemini.

Safety rule:
The backend calls Gemini. Do not expose GEMINI_API_KEY in frontend code.

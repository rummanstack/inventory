StockLedger SEO Next Steps
=========================

Goal
----
Make StockLedger easier to discover for searches related to inventory, POS, accounting, HR, payroll, DSR, dealer management, pharmacy management, grocery store management, wholesale business software, and business software in Bangladesh.

1. Prerender Public Pages
-------------------------
Current public pages are React client-rendered. Google can render React, but prerendered HTML is stronger for SEO.

Prerender these pages:
- /landing
- /features
- /features/inventory-management
- /features/retail-pos
- /features/accounting
- /features/hr-payroll
- /features/purchase-management

- /solutions/pharmacy
- /solutions/grocery-store
- /pricing
- /contact
- /get-started
- /founder
- /privacy-policy
- /terms

2. Replace Generic Images With Real Product Screenshots
-------------------------------------------------------
Feature pages should show real StockLedger product screens. Users and Google both trust real product evidence more than generic visuals.

Use real screenshots for:
- Accounting: ledger, trial balance, P&L, balance sheet
- POS: quick sale, invoice, receipt
- Inventory: products, stock movement, low-stock alerts
- DSR: morning issue, evening settlement, shop due ledger
- HR & Payroll: employees, attendance, leave, payroll
- Installment Sales: plan detail, schedule, overdue report
- Purchases: purchase receive, supplier statement
- Reporting: dashboard, profit report, daily sales report

Keep lifestyle/business images mainly for solution pages.

3. Write Stronger Long-Form Page Copy
-------------------------------------
Each important SEO page should have:
- One clear H1
- 800-1500 words of useful content
- Real screenshots
- 5-8 FAQs
- Internal links to related pages
- One clear CTA

Priority pages to expand first:

5. Add More Schema Markup
-------------------------
Current schema is a good start. Add:
- FAQPage schema for FAQ sections
- BreadcrumbList schema
- SoftwareApplication schema with pricing details
- Organization schema with social links
- LocalBusiness schema if StockLedger has a public business location/profile

6. Improve Core Web Vitals
--------------------------
Many current images are large PNG files. Large images can hurt rankings and conversions.

Targets:
- Hero images under 250 KB
- Screenshots under 300-500 KB
- Convert heavy PNGs to WebP or AVIF
- Lazy-load below-fold images
- Preload only the main hero image
- Keep mobile layout fast and stable

7. Set Up Google Search Console
-------------------------------
After deployment:
- Submit https://stockledger.pro/sitemap.xml
- Request indexing for the most important pages
- Monitor indexed pages weekly
- Watch queries with impressions but low CTR
- Improve titles/descriptions for low CTR pages

Priority URLs to request indexing:
- /landing
- /features/accounting
- /features/retail-pos
- /features/inventory-management
- /features/dsr-dealer-management
- /solutions/dealer-distributor
- /solutions/retail-shop
- /pricing
- /get-started

8. Build Authority and Trust
----------------------------
Code alone cannot guarantee top rankings. StockLedger also needs trust signals.

Add:
- Real customer testimonials
- Real business screenshots
- Case studies from shops, dealers, pharmacies, and wholesalers
- Google Business Profile
- Social profiles linked from footer
- Local business directory listings
- Backlinks from Bangladeshi SME/business websites
- Blog posts about inventory, accounting, POS, DSR, and retail operations

Best Next Technical Task
------------------------
Implement prerendering, FAQ schema, Breadcrumb schema, and an image optimization pipeline.

This is the strongest next technical SEO upgrade after the current foundation.
